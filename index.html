Low tether.
Less rediscovery.
Faster revival.

STATIC FIRSTKeep idle infrastructure small.Local-first and edge-delivered interfaces.
PROCESS MEMORYReduce the cost of restarting.Changelogs, skills and tests preserve context.
MANAGED SERVICESBuy reliability, not maintenance.Vercel and Supabase remove undifferentiated work.
SELECTIVE AISpend models on ambiguity.Use deterministic software for everything else.
